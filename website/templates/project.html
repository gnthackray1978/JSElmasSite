The Strad - latest issue

To celebrate the 125th anniversary of The Strad magazine. I redesigned the magazine to reflect the changes made in the industry. My brief was to create a clean, luxurious, coherent design that enhances the content and gain a better user experience. I introduced new fonts to create dynamic typographic headlines and sections. Lines in various weights and styles are used to help aid the creative design and mirror the stringed theme.
